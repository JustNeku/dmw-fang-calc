Information

How does this work exactly?

Input your values: Starting Money Amount, what you predict you have to pay for resources, etc.

Add what you already have on you.

The calculator does the following:

- Buy your first Ultimate Fang Piece

- With the Voucher you obtain craft the same piece again

- Sell it to get a partial return of investment

- Repeat until all Gear pieces are bought.

The last two steps are optional, they get you some money back.
